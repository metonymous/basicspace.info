BasicSpace, located in inner Melbourne suburbia, Australia, is a community space for creative technolites, where they can practice, experiment and refine their crafts in an comfortable environment.

Membership Levels

We provide services in tiers, with a nominal fee to access the higher level services of the space.

Each tier includes all the facilities of lower tiers, except where they are superceded.

Note: Speeds and downloads listed are maximums and may not always be available.

Guest (Free)

  • Non permanent (shared) user accounts to all enduser machines.
  • 128kbps wired internet.
  • 100mb/day.
  • Office hours access.

User ($5/year)

  • Permanent user accounts on all enduser machines.
  • Guest access to server machines.
  • Hosted email (google apps).
  • 256kbps internet.
  • Wireless access.
  • Toilet privilages.
  • 1gb/day.
  • Chat account (google apps).
  • Image storage account (picasa web).
  • Single subdomais (username.basicspace.info)

PowerUser ($20/year)

  • own computer, stored on site

Other Services

In addition to the to the standard memberships, we also offer other services, including classes, short courses and one on one tuition.

Topics will be added as teachers come available and as demand necessitates. There is a basic fee of $10 per 90 minute class, with teachers and tutors often making themselves available after this time elapses.

Private tuition is available from $50/hour, although some tutors offer pro-bono tutoring on some subjects to some students. Feel free to drop in, introduce yourself, and get to know the tutors.
