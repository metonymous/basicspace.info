For paying members BasicSpace can be booked in advance, which will ensure that there is room for you in the space.

Group bookings

Parties, functions, competitions, gaming sessions, hack-a-thons... the list is endless.

Booking the entire venue carries a $50 deposit (per day booked) which you get back at the end of the event.

Normal access rules apply, but when booked for a function, special privilages (such as kitchen, bathroom, furniture re-arrangment) can be allotted.
